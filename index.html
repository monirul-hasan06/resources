About Us

This platform was created only our section with a simple mission: to make academic life easier and more organized for friends. We understand how challenging it can be to manage class notes, track CT, mid-term, and exam schedules, calculate CGPA and prepare lab reports at the same time. That is why we built this website as a central hub where all these important academic resources are available in one place.

Our website provides reliable class notes, up-to-date exam information, easy-to-use CGPA calculation tools, and helpful lab report guidelines designed specifically for students. This is a completely non-commercial initiative developed to support friends and classmates, not for profit. We believe that when students help one another, everyone grows stronger academically.

Our goal is to create a student-friendly environment that saves time, reduces confusion, and encourages collaborative learning — because learning together is the best way to succeed together.
